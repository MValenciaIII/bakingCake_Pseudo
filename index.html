Baking a Cake

STEP by STEP. READ FROM LEFT TO RIGHT.

Ingredients

  • First, we need to get unsalted butter from the fridge on top of the fridge.
  • The eggs are in the very bottom shelf of the fridge and grab that as well.
  • Next, grab the buttermilk next to it and place it on the counter next to the oven.
  • Next, we will need unsweetened cocoa powder from the cabinet above the ingredients you've placed.
  • The flour and baking powder are next to each other, and a shelf above where the cocoa powder was.
  • Grab the salt, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract at the very top shelf.
  • place all those ingredients next to the eggs.

Materials

  • Look in the drawer under you, grab the keychain of measuring cups, ,and place it on the counter.
  • Go back into the drawer and grab the whisk.
  • Look under that drawer, open the cabinet, grab the cake pan, and large silver mixing bowl.
  • As well as grab a small silver mixing bowl and place it next to the ingredients.

Preparing oven and ingredients

  • Walk to the oven, next to the ingredients, and look at the buttons.
  • Click the button that says pre-heat and click the numbers 3-5-0.
  • Now grab the cake pan, it's long one.
  • grab the vegetable oil and 3 paper towel behind you.
  • fold the 3 paper towels together, and grab the bottle of vegetable oil and turn it over slowly until theres a little bit of oil on the paper towels.
  • Put the vegetable oil bottle down and wipe the paper towel on the cake pan from side to side.
  • open the flour bag and grab a little more than a pinch full and spread it all over the pan.

Mixing wet ingredients

  • Now we are going to place the "wet ingredients" in the large mixing bowl.
  • Grab the keychain of measuring cups.
  • Get the unsalted butter and put enough to fill up the 3/4 cup. Then put it in the mixing bowl.
  • Get one teaspoon of vanilla extract and pour it in as well.
  • fill 1 cup of granulated sugar and pour it in the large mixing bowl.
  • Then fill 1/2 a cup of buttermilk and pour it in.
  • Grab 3 eggs from the egg carton and crack the shell.
  • After cracking the egg pour the egg into the mixing bowl.
  • Then grab the whisk and mix it altogether til they look well mixed.

Mixing Dry ingredients

  • We are going to mix these ingredients in a seperate bowl and they will be called "dry ingridients".
  • Grab the small silver mixing bowl and well get 3/4 of a cup of flour and put it in.
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt is next, 3/4 of a cup of cocoa powder is put in there as well, and 1/2 a teaspoon of baking powder
  • Grab the whisk and mix the small bowl.
  • Then pour the small silver bowl into the large bowl.
  • Then finally mix the the large bowl once again til mixed well.

Procceeding to finish.

  • After mixing, Pour the large bowl into cake pan.
  • Then after pouring all of the batter grab the cake pan by the handles and put in the oven
  • Wait 15 minutes for the oven.
  • Then rotate the pan and wait 15 more minutes.
  • Take out after 30 minutes have passed and let it cool.
  • Enjoy!
